Community Guidelines

This is a safe space. To protect everyone, please follow these guidelines:

  • Be Kind: Everyone is fighting a battle you know nothing about.
  • Respect Privacy: Don't share personal information about yourself or others.
  • No Judgement: We're here to support, not to criticize.
  • Trigger Warnings: Please add "TW:" before content that might be triggering.
  • No Promotion: This isn't a place for advertising products or services.
  • Professional Advice: We support each other, but we're not a substitute for professional help.
  • Report Concerns: Use the report button if you see anything concerning.

All stories are moderated to ensure safety and respect.
