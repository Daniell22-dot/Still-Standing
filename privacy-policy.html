Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 10, 2026

1. Introduction

At STILL STANDING, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you register for an account, we collect:

  • Name (optional for anonymous story sharing)
  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted and stored securely)
  • Phone number (optional, for counseling services)

2.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on the platform
  • Interaction with stories and resources

2.3 Content You Create

  • Stories shared on the platform
  • Comments and interactions with other users' stories
  • Booking information for counseling sessions
  • Progress tracker data (mood entries, self-care activities)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Services: Create and manage your account, facilitate counseling bookings, and enable story sharing
  • Improve Platform: Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience and develop new features
  • Communication: Send important updates, appointment reminders, and support resources
  • Safety: Monitor for harmful content and ensure community guidelines are followed
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations and protect against fraud

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. Your data is only shared in the following circumstances:

  • Counselors: Session booking details are shared with assigned counselors only
  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party services for hosting, analytics, and email delivery
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect safety (e.g., imminent risk of harm)
  • Anonymous Stories: Approved stories are published on the platform; anonymous authors remain unidentified

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Secure password storage with hashing
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication for admin functions
  • Secure database hosting with MySQL

Note: While we take extensive measures to protect your data, no system is 100% secure. Please use strong passwords and keep your account credentials confidential.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your login session
  • Remember your preferences (e.g., theme, daily verse progress)
  • Analyze site traffic and user behavior
  • Improve platform performance

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling them may affect platform functionality.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Data Portability: Export your data in a readable format
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Anonymity: Share stories anonymously without revealing your identity

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@stillstanding.com

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. When you delete your account, we remove your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required for legal, safety, or operational purposes (e.g., published anonymous stories).

9. Children's Privacy

STILL STANDING is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we can remove it.

10. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to external websites (e.g., support resources). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites. Please review their privacy policies before sharing personal information.

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a notice on the platform. Your continued use of STILL STANDING after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact:

  • Privacy Team: privacy@stillstanding.com
  • Support: support@stillstanding.com
  • Hotline: +254 112 219135

Your privacy matters to us. We are committed to protecting your information and providing a safe, supportive space.
