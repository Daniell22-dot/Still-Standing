Touch a Child's Heart

Dedicated support for our youngest community members.

Our Commitment to Children

Children experience trauma and grief differently. Our "Touch a Child's Heart" initiative focuses on creating safe, child-friendly spaces for healing, protection, and emotional growth.

Creative Healing

Using art, play, and storytelling to help children express complex emotions they can't put into words.

Safe Schools

Outreach programs to identify and support children experiencing abuse or neglect in educational settings.

Mentorship

Pairing older youth and mentors with children who have lost parents or primary caregivers.
