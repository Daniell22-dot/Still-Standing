Child Protection & Support

Protecting our children, securing our future.

Child Helpline Kenya

Report abuse, neglect, or exploitation. Free 24/7.

116

Speak up. You might be the only one who can help.

Signs of Abuse

  • Unexplained injuries (bruises, burns, cuts)
  • Withdrawal from friends or usual activities
  • Changes in behavior (aggression, depression)
  • Fear of going home or seeing specific people
  • Changes in eating or sleeping habits

How to Help

  • Listen: If a child talks to you, listen without judgment.
  • Reassure: Let them know it's not their fault.
  • Report: Call 116 or report to the nearest police station/Gender Desk.
  • Support: Connect them with professional help.
