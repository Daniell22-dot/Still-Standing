From Pain to Purpose

My name is Daniel Manyasa, and this is why I started STILL STANDING

In 2018, I lost my grandmother - the woman who raised me. The grief was overwhelming, but it led me to a powerful realization: no one should have to suffer alone.

Today, STILL STANDING has grown into a community of thousands supporting each other through grief, addiction recovery, and mental health challenges.

"Your pain can become your purpose. Your struggle can become your strength. Your story can become someone else's survival guide."

— Daniel Manyasa, Founder
