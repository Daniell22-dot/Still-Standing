Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service really free? +

Yes, absolutely. STILL STANDING provides all support services free of charge. We believe that mental health support should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial situation.

Is my information kept confidential? +

Yes, confidentiality is our top priority. We never share your personal information with anyone outside our trained support team. The only exception would be if we believe there's immediate risk of harm to yourself or others, in which case we may need to contact emergency services.

What qualifications do your counselors have? +

Our team consists of trained peer counselors who have personal experience with grief, addiction recovery, or mental health challenges. Many are certified in peer support counseling. However, we are not licensed therapists or psychiatrists. For clinical diagnoses or medication management, we can provide referrals to appropriate professionals.

Can I remain anonymous? +

Yes, you can choose to remain anonymous when contacting us. For phone and WhatsApp support, we don't require your real name. For online forms, you can use initials or a pseudonym if you prefer.

What if I need help outside of Kenya? +

While we're based in Kenya, we provide online support to anyone worldwide. Our WhatsApp and online sessions are available internationally. However, for local emergency services or in-person support, we recommend contacting services in your own country.

How can I support STILL STANDING? +

There are several ways to support us: 1) Share our resources with someone who might need them, 2) Volunteer as a peer supporter (training provided), 3) Share your story to help others, 4) Make a donation to help cover operational costs, 5) Follow and share our social media posts.
